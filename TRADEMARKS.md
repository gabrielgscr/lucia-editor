If you create and distribute a modified version or fork of this project:

- you must not use the original branding in a way that causes confusion

- you should use a different name, logo, and visual identity for your fork
- you may state that your fork is "based on Lucia Editor" or "derived from Lucia Editor" as a factual description

Examples:
- Allowed: "MyEditorX is based on Lucia Editor"
